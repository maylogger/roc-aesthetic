Generate a legacy-style 「機關資訊儀表板」or intranet homepage (not SaaS KPI dashboard).

Requirements:
- 2–3 narrow columns plus optional sticky shortcut rail
- many small KPI numbers as plain text or cramped table cells—not giant spark charts
- quick links grouped with different heading styles（像不同年份改版疊上去）
- status strip: 「系統維護公告」「請勿於離峰時間大量查詢」 such tone
- multiple bordered panels; some with gray header bars
- ROC palette accents; cyan links; occasional red 「緊急」 tag
- slightly misaligned titles vs content padding

Avoid:
Glassmorphism, Inter-only typography, roomy card grid dashboards.
